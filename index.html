Before you begin

Local-first

Reports and your identity are stored in this browser only. There's no account, no server-side storage, no cloud sync.

You're in control of transfers

Data moves between devices only when you actively scan or share a QR code. Each transfer is a deliberate action.

Peer-to-peer

Devices connect directly — your data never passes through an intermediary server. The default mode uses a free PeerJS signaling server just to help the two devices find each other; once connected, data flows directly. Your data is encrypted end-to-end by WebRTC regardless of which transport is used.

Stays on this device

Your identity and reports are stored here only. They're removed if you clear browser data or uninstall this app. For longest-term storage, install this as an app.

A prototype from TU Delft

This is a demonstration. Treat it as a working draft rather than a production tool.
