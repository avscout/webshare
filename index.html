Before you begin

Local-first

Sessions and your identity are stored in this browser only. There's no account, no server-side storage, no cloud sync.

You're in control of transfers

Data moves between devices only when you actively scan or share a QR code. Each transfer is a deliberate action.

Peer-to-peer

Devices connect directly. With "PeerJS" mode, a public signaling server (peerjs.com) helps the two devices find each other, but your data never passes through it. With "Raw WebRTC" mode, no server is involved at all.

Stays on this device

Your identity and sessions are stored here only. They're removed if you clear browser data or uninstall this app. For longest-term storage, install this as an app.

A prototype from TU Delft

This is a demonstration. Treat it as a working draft rather than a production tool.
